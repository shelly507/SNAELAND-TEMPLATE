    The Lavender Dreams Church

    Located in the heart of the scenic town of Vik, the Lavender Dreams Church is a place of worship, tranquility, and community. Experience the beauty of its architecture and the rich history of the local culture. Come and join us for mass, or visit for a peaceful moment of prayer and reflection.

    Service Schedule:

    • Sunday Mass: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM
    • Weekday Services: Monday to Friday, 6:00 PM
    • Special Events: Christmas Eve Mass, Easter Sunday Service, and more!

    Visiting Hours:

    Monday - Saturday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

    Closed on Sundays except for services.
